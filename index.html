Chicken soup is my favorite simple Recipe, because it is easy to made, with simple home Ingredients and in very short time. This is good for health. You can eat it any time You want. Chicken soup is a soup made from chicken, simmered in water, usually with various other ingredients. The classic Chicken soup consists of a clear chicken broth, often with pieces of chicken or vegetables; common additions are pasta, noodles, dumplings, or grains such as rice and barley. (Wikipedia)

# Ingredients

  • 1 (3 pound) whole chicken
  • 4 carrots, halved
  • 4 stalks celery, halved
  • 1 large onion, halved
  • water to cover
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon chicken bouillon granul(optional)

# Directions

  1. Put the chicken, carrots, celery and onion in a large soup pot and cover with cold water. üç≤
  2. Heat and simmer, uncovered, until the chicken meat falls off of the bones (skim off foam every so often) ü•£
  3. Take everything out of the pot.
  4. Strain the broth. Pick the meat off of the bones and chop the carrots, celery and onion.ü•ó
  5. Season the broth with salt, pepper and chicken bouillon to taste, if desired. üç±
  6. Return the chicken, carrots, celery and onion to the pot, stir together, and serve.üçú
